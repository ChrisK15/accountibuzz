# Plan 03.1-01 — Admin Realtime Review Queue & Badge

## What shipped

A new client-side Realtime patcher (`useReviewQueueRealtime`) that closes the
`03-VERIFICATION.md` §"Phase 3.1 Follow-Up Items" item 2 gap: when Device B
submits a fresh photo/video, Device A's admin pending-review badge AND swipe
queue at `/groups/[id]/review` auto-update within ~2s with no manual back-out
+ re-enter.

The wire was already lit on the DB side (Phase 3 publication + Phase 4 migration
`20260508233129` for `replica identity full`); the missing piece was the
client-side hook to invalidate the two TanStack Query caches that feed the
badge and the queue list.

## Decisions applied

- **D-01 (channel naming)** — `review-queue:${groupId}:${mountPoint}` per Q1
  resolution from CONTEXT.md (per-mount-point channel so the badge and the
  list each get their own subscription, avoiding the dual-mount cleanup race
  that bit Phase 3).
- **D-02 (predicate narrowing)** — `filter: 'group_id=eq.{groupId}'` on the
  postgres_changes binding, plus an in-handler `status === 'pending'` narrowing
  before invalidating, so transitions OUT of pending also invalidate the
  badge/queue (status updates don't add to the queue but they DO remove from
  it).
- **D-03 (cache key)** — invalidates BOTH `['reviewQueue', groupId]` and
  `['pendingReviewCount', groupId]` on every relevant event; matches the two
  consumers' existing TanStack queryKeys.
- **D-04 (admin gate at the mount point)** — both mount points pass `undefined`
  as `groupId` when `isAdmin` is false, and the hook exits early on
  `undefined`. No subscription is opened for non-admin members.
